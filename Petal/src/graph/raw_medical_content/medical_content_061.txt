What is amenorrhea?

Amenorrhea means not having a period. It is normal for some girls to not start their periods until age 16. But you should see your doctor if you have not started your period by 15. You should also see your doctor if you have started your period but it then stops for more than 3 months. Read Amenorrhea: Absence of Periods to learn more.
Your doctor may ask questions or do tests to find out the cause of your amenorrhea. If you have had sex and you have missed your period, you should have a pregnancy test.
